To post a key/authenticate:
curl -H "Accept: application/json" -H "Content-type: application/json" -X POST -d '{"username":"bcrowthe", "password":"unb password"}' -c cookie-jar http://info3103.cs.unb.ca:43005/signin

To get a key:
curl -i -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X GET -b cookie-jar http://info3103.cs.unb.ca:43005/signin
